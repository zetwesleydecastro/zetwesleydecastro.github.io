Top Attractions Near Mount Mayon

Discover the most visited and beautiful spots surrounding the majestic volcano.

Cagsawa Ruins

Famous remnants of a 16th-century church destroyed by a volcanic eruption, offering a dramatic view of Mayon in the background.

Ligñon Hill

A popular viewpoint and adventure park, perfect for ziplining, hiking, and panoramic shots of Mount Mayon.

Daraga Church

A baroque-style church built in the 1700s with a scenic location overlooking the volcano — a mix of heritage and nature.

Sumlang Lake

Relax on a bamboo raft and take in the peaceful waters with Mayon as your majestic backdrop. Instagram-worthy views guaranteed!

Mayon ATV Adventure

Ride through lava trails and rugged terrain on an ATV for a thrilling experience at the base of the volcano!
