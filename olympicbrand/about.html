About the Olympic Brand

The Olympic Brand represents the values, history, and global spirit of the Olympic Movement. It symbolizes unity, excellence, and respect across nations.

Learn more at the Official Olympic Brand Page.

Olympic Rings

The five interlaced rings represent the five continents and symbolize the unity of athletes worldwide.

Olympic Colors

The six colors (blue, yellow, black, green, red, white) were chosen because every nation's flag includes at least one.

Olympic Motto

"Citius, Altius, Fortius" means "Faster, Higher, Stronger," inspiring athletes to reach their full potential.

Brand History

The Olympic symbol and brand identity have evolved since 1913, remaining a global mark of excellence and unity.

Brand Design

The brand guidelines ensure consistent use of logos, colors, typography, and imagery worldwide.

Fun Fact

The Olympic rings were first used at the 1920 Antwerp Olympics. Today, they are recognized worldwide.
